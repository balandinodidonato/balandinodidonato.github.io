Il Sogno Nel Sogno

Il Sogno nel sogno, for tape and Metis, has been composed and performed by Baladino Di Donato in 2013 as part of his BMus diseration at Conservatorio Alfredo Casella dell'Aquila.
Il Sogno nel sogno has been premired on the same year at Auditorium del Castello (L'Aquila, Italy) designed by the architect Renzo Piano.

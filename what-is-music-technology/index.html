What is music technology?


Introduction

This lecture introduces you to the world of music technologies. You will learn what they are, how they used, the different contexts in which they are used (recording, production, composition, performance), and how these can be used to overcome societal challenges such as diversity and inclusion.
The lecture is delivered on 22 March 2021, at 12:15 online on Zoom. Please, use the link that was sent to you.
Please, get in touch for any question, issue, feedback, comment, chat, or... anything really!
